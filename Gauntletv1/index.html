WHATS? > The Gauntlet

A DE-FI TOOL WHICH ENRICHES THE LIQUIDITY PROVISIONING EXPERIENCE

The Gauntlet platform is the first decentralized finance tool that facilitates both traditional and multi-party liquidity provisioning with yield farming support for fungible and non-fungible tokens.

The Gauntlet platform decentralizes traditional concepts such as the certificate of deposit and the interest rate swap in a non-custodial and rewarding way which brings unrivalled opportunities to liquidity providers.

THE OVERALL

PROJECT SUMMARY

The Gauntlet platform is the first decentralized finance tool to facilitate both traditional and multi-party liquidity provisioning with yield farming support for both fungible and non-fungible tokens.

Moreover, it is the first tool to pioneer token reward swaps, enabling pending token rewards from liquidity provisioning to be swapped or collateralized prematurely.
The project is extremely experimental but it has been moving fast in terms of progress ever since its inception and it is now gradually moving out of stealth mode.
